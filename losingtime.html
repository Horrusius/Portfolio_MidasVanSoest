Losing Time - Immersive Design

This is the result of my Communication and Multimedia Design graduation project.

During this project I worked with the artists known by the name ELLER. I was tasked to make a stage experience for their newest ablum; Losing Time. The biggest obstacle within this project was finding a way to create interaction within the concert hall without using the artist themselves to activate the audience.

Oger - Immersive Design

This is the result of a 5 week long project for Oger.

Their renovated store is also going to change their brand astethic. They are going for Oger 2.0 and to add to this, they wanted a product that could help them make the store more interesting for a slightly younger audience. That is why we created a media presenting suit setup, to keep the original seriousness and classic feeling while also adding a multimedia element to it.

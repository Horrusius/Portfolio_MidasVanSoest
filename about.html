About Me

I am an energetic and enthousiastic designer from Amsterdam, Netherlands. I specialize in Immersive Environmental Design and User Experience Design and have a large affinity with 3D art and animation.

My interests are all over the place. Graphic design, webdesign, UX/UI, 3D, 2D, game design, animation, immersive design, visual design, art, music. fashion and so much more. I'm always looking at different things to draw inspiration from or new ways to express myself. I've tried many different things and I'll keep looking for new things to try and learn about, simply because I think it's fun. I use all of this accumulated knowlegde in my work, to create the right experiences for every situation.

I graduated from the University of Applied sciences in the field of Communication & Multimedia Design in 2023.

Beyond - Product Design

The goal of this project was to create a digital solution to a problem that goes 'beyond' the screen. So, in other words, we were not allowed to use a screen within our design. My team decided to tackle a problem students could run into.

I worked on the following parts within this project:

Deskresearch, Designchallange and subquestions, Ideation session, Storyboard, Customer journey and the animation.

This project is in Dutch.

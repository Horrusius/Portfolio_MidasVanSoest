Thunderboom Records - Virtual Artist Fi - Immersive Design

During my internship at Thunderboom Records I got to work on their Virtual Artist project. For this project I was to make interactive/reactive concepts for their live performance called Call & Response. This live performance was a collabaration between Thunderboom Records' Virtual Artist Fi, the rapper and spoken word artist Kay Slice and D-Sound.
